SevenBits is the online alias of a unique but somewhat quirky programmer from New Hampshire. He has been coding for years and has many projects to his name, some of which as so old that recollection will be a challenge.

He writes commercial software for the Mac, is the author of a somewhat popular (in niche circles) software program called Mac Linux USB Loader and its associated firmware-to-be, Enterprise. Fluent in Java, Objective-C, C, and some C++, he has also dabbled in HTML5 and WebGL, which he considers to be very cool (from a technical standpoint, but also great for building web-based first person shooters).

If you need anything, feel free to contact me.
